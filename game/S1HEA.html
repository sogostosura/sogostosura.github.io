Car Crash Test

Car Crash is an exciting game with realistic physics and excellent three—dimensional graphics, in which you have to test the strength of the car by breaking it in various ways! • Crash cars • Get coins for it • Buy cars and cards • Crash cars again! The machines are controlled by standard WASD keys or arrows on the keyboard. • C - change the camera view - In the standard first view, you can rotate the camera with the mouse. • Space - handbrake • Left Shift - Nitro (for advertising) There are buttons on the game screen: • Level reset (R key) • Main menu (H key) • Time dilation (T key) - It is more convenient to perform these actions from the keyboard, but you can also click on the buttons with the mouse. - To make the mouse visible - press the Escape or TAB key.
